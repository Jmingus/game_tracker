Quick Play helps you choose a game to play with your friends.

Here's how it works:

  1. Enter how many players you've got.
  2. Then, enter how much time you have to play, in minutes.
  3. Quick Play will look through your Vault and choose a game that meets those criteria. If you don't want to play the game shown, click next to see another match.
